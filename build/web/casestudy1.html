    What Is Salesforce?

    Salesforce, Inc. is an American cloud-based software company that creates meaningful customer experiences through software and services. Salesforce is a central customer relationship management (CRM) solution used by support, sales, and marketing teams worldwide. It began as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) offering with a multi-tenant design that supports API interaction, configuration, and scalability. Benefits include free functionality, low cost of ownership, platform support, and more.

    Salesforce services enable organisations to better engage with partners, customers, and potential customers by utilising cloud technology. It is one of the leading Customer Relationship Management (CRM) service providers and an Artificial Intelligence (AI) platform for marketing automation, financial management, human resource management, and so on.
